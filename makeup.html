Beautiful makeup is the key to self-confidence, so it is doubly important for a photo shoot. The camera never captures color exactly as we see it in real life. Therefore, the makeup for the photo shoot should be a little more intense than that which is done in everyday life. Even if you want your face to look as natural as possible in the photo, you still need to exaggerate the makeup you put on, because it won't show through the camera as much.

The main difference between makeup for a photo shoot and normal makeup is that only matte foundation should be used for the photo. Also in the makeup should be drawn clear lines and carefully shaded shadows, tone and blush.
